Be Mindful of Fake or Lookalike Websites

Cyber criminals sometimes create websites that look almost identical to official ones, but with small differences in the web address. These are designed to trick you into giving away personal information.

Example:

✅ www.cranleigh.org

❌ www.cranliegh.org

How to Protect Yourself

  • Always check the spelling of the website address before entering your login details.
  • Bookmark the official website: www.cranleigh.org.
  • Look for https:// and a padlock symbol in the address bar.
  • Be cautious with links from unexpected emails, texts, or social media posts.

Remember:

Our official site is always www.cranleigh.org — double-check before you click!
